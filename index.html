About Us

Designed with our children in mind, this line is a mix of eco friendly fabrics & innovative artistic designs serving as an eclectic alternative in kid's clothing. All goods are hand rendered and printed on eco friendly fabrics using non-toxic inks in our NY based studio. Each piece comes with a small seeded hangtag to plant with your child and teach them from now about living green.

Our Mission:

If there was one thing we were certain about as a company was that we wanted to make a difference. Our constant concern for the environment and all beings in it were the driving force behind our two very special collections, Endangered Species Collection and Give & Care Collection.

To a better tomorrow...
